The Finance Olympiad is a special curated Olympiad, made with a purpose to inculcate the habit and increase the knowledge of personal finances in a fun way. This is an online multiple-choice question-based Olympiad in which graduates and undergraduates can participate. This Olympiad can be very helpful for working professionals too.


You should apply if you are:

  • Want to gain financial knowledge.
  • Get an insight into managing personal finances in a better way.
  • Keen to test your aptitude skills.
  • Want to enhance your resume/portfolio.

Registration of Students: The Finance Olympiad is open to all. This Olympiad is designed in such a way that it will help in a way or the other. This Olympiad provides a win-win situation for all the candidates, that can yield fruitful results. Upon successful registration, Springfield Olympiads will register the applicant and the applicant will receive the email regarding guidelines and login credentials for the examination. Syllabus:

  • The Olympiad will be a multiple choice question online olympiad.
  • There will be a total of 30 questions in the Olympiad based on personal finances.
  • The duration of Olympiad is of 1 hour only.
