SISO


The Springfield International Science Olympiad (SISO), a test of competence and knowledge in the subject of Science, is held based on syllabus prescribed by CBSE/ ICSE and the State Boards.

Finance Olympiad


The Finance Olympiad is a special curated Olympiad, made with a purpose to inculcate the habit and increase the knowledge of personal finances in a fun way. This is an online multiple-choice question-based Olympiad in which graduates and undergraduates can participate.
